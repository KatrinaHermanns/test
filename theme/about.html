”Punctuation Modifier”

/ˌpəNG(k)(t)SHəˈwāSH(ə)n ˈmädəˌfī(ə)r/

Noun

A person who constantly challenges the question mark behind possibility through creative, outside of the box thinking. Someone who changes possibility into the mark of reality through endless drive and passion.

Synonyms: Katrina Hermanns


Besides being a book worm,tea enthusiast and kick boxing aficionado I am extremely passionate about challenging the definition of “Impossible”. I first found this passion in middle school when teachers would question my plans for extravagant projects, such as recreating a Mars sandstorm inside the classroom and collecting 3000 pop cans to build a chair. In high school I continued to create unexpected projects, which included creating my own process for extracting biofuel. This “why not?” attitude led me to studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo.
