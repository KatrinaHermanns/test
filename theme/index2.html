MY PROJECTS & IDEAS









Sustainable Biofuel from Algae Project

Throughout my senior year in high school I developed a natural and sustainable way to extract biofuel from algae, without using any industrial chemicals or machines.This was a small scale production experiment which required me to research and develop the process myself. By the end of the project I produced biofuel that would power a small deisle engine.


Theremin Project

In 2017 I had the opportunity to build a theremin for the Toronto based band, Strawman. I was required to research and select a design, present the design and a quote to the band, and then build the individual circuits, components and housing for the theremin. This project allowed me to learn how to design and build a circuit, solder, source parts and design a timeline that worked for both my team and the client.









Personal Website

During my first co-op term, I decided to extend my basic HTML knowledge and build a personal website. Using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap (with a shout out to glyphicon!) I built this site to allow potential employers to learn a bit more about me.
